The Blink Design System

One brand across four apps — Customer, Rider, Agent and Merchant. Bright, rounded and confident: a single raspberry pink doing the heavy lifting over cool slate neutrals. Built for the Algerian market, in DZD.

Foundations

Brand

The wordmark is the pink "B + lightning-bolt" mark beside black geometric "LINK." lettering — the bolt signals speed. The mark uses currentColor, so it recolors freely: pink on white, white on brand.

Color

One dominant color — Blink raspberry — over a cool slate neutral ramp. Pale-pink tints wash info surfaces and icon halos. A teal "credit" accent marks positive money; a spring-green secondary appears only on About Us. Used boldly but sparingly: surfaces stay white, pink punches through.
